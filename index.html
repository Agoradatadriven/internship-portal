Internship Program

Official Framework & Agreement

1.0 Application Process Summary

This internship is execution-focused. It is designed for applicants who are willing to learn through real work, follow systems, and grow into a strong support role in lead generation and paid media operations. We value attitude, reliability, and attention to detail as much as prior experience.

  • Step 1: Application Submission - Submit your resume, short introduction, availability, and answers to the application questions.
  • Step 2: Initial Screening and Assessment Task - Shortlisted applicants will complete a short practical task.
  • Step 3: Interview, Final Decision, and Internship Offer - Finalists will be interviewed before the final decision is made.

2.0 Role Summary & Basics

This internship is designed to train and evaluate someone for a potential long-term role in our Lead Generation Team. The team supports media buying, Meta Ads, Google Ads, lead generation campaigns, and creative execution.

What the Intern Will Help With:

  • Campaign setup support
  • Creative editing (Canva, CapCut)
  • QA and checklist support
  • Reporting updates
  • Funnel and lead flow checks
  • File and asset organization
  • Basic campaign operations support

What the Intern Will Not Own Yet:

  • Campaign strategy
  • Budget decisions
  • Client communication
  • Final launch approvals
  • Major optimization decisions
  • Unsupervised live account changes

3.0 What We Look For in Applicants

We are not only looking for experience. We are mainly looking for people who show:

  • Reliability
  • Attention to detail
  • Coachability
  • Clear communication
  • Strong work ethic
  • Willingness to learn

4.0 Payment Structure

  • Compensation rate: ₱350 per day
  • Payment basis: per day attended/worked
  • Internship duration: 2 months
  • Estimated monthly allowance: approximately ₱7,700/month based on 22 working days. Actual monthly total will depend on the number of working days attended.

The internship allowance is intended to support interns during the training and execution period. Payment is based on actual attendance and completed internship working days. Absences, unpaid leave, or unworked days will not be included unless otherwise approved. The internship allowance does not guarantee regular employment after the program.

5.0 Career Ladder

Interns who successfully complete the internship and meet performance standards may be considered for a junior role. The team structure progresses as follows:

  • 1. Lead Generation Team Intern - Learns the workflow and supports execution.
  • 2. Junior Lead Generation Team Member - Handles recurring campaign and operations work under supervision.
  • 3. Department Head / Team Lead - Leads execution quality, workflow, and team output.
  • 4. Client Account Manager - Owns client communication, expectations, and account direction.

6.0 Confidentiality and Data Handling

Client data, account access, reports, files, and internal processes are confidential.

  • Do not share, copy, forward, or reuse company or client materials outside approved channels.
  • Do not use personal accounts unless approved.
  • Do not make live changes in accounts without approval.
